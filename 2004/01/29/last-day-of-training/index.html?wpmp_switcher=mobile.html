Last day of training

The training if for over 4 days, and this is the last day. Thank god things went smooth! Just hoping that the feedback from the students is good too….. my appreciation award depends on that!

I was planning to visit singapore for a day by advancing my flight to singapore by 1 day and get a transit visa… but to my shock I discovered that transit visas are not given by singapore immigration for indians.. and that’s coz they have had a history of indians taking the transit visa and getting lost into the crowd…. jeez, it sad! I really cant blame them for that, at the same time, I deserve better!I got a business visa, that too by my company and I have a confirmed ticket to India…. if I were to have enough money to confirm a ticket that i wont be claiming, then what do I need to get lost as a illegal immigrant for!

This sux :(
