Lots happening, yet nothing happening

That’s exactly how it has been for the past fortnight. It is one of those times when the entire universe seems to going at its usual pace and you are being left behind. It’s like a time warp, you the entire world clicking away like a clock, and you are an outsider observing it, trying not to interfere. This has been the way in my work as well. Very little actually got done in the last 15 days.

My mind has been a uneasily calm, yet chaotic at the same time. The calmness is that of inability to take decisions, and being forced to play a waiting game. The chaos is the desparity of things not being in my control, having to wait for things to happen, and then having to react to them. I was so lost in thoughts that I failed to see time slip by. This is time travel :)
